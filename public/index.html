WHO AM I?

Hello! I'm Jennifer Yang!

I am a full-stack software engineer with a healthcare background.

Previously, I tackled problems in healthcare as a nurse. As I personally witnessed my peers struggling with software, I wanted to find solutions for more efficient user experiences. My curiosity for programming and creativity pushed me to make the leap into development where I attended the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy. Now, you can always find me drinking a cup of coffee or milk tea as I'm discovering new technologies.

When I am not coding, I am an avid fitness enthusiast and traveller. I enjoy yoga, kickboxing, and playing volleyball during my free time!
